Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Orders placed before 3:00 PM are delivered same day. Orders after 3:00 PM are delivered the next day. Delivery typically takes 2-4 hours during business hours.

What is your minimum order?

We have a minimum order of ₦2,500 for delivery to ensure efficient service.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify your order if we haven't started processing it. Please contact us immediately at 0805 726 6953.

Do you deliver outside Abuja?

Currently, we only deliver within Abuja and surrounding areas. Contact us for special arrangements.

What if I'm not available for delivery?

Please provide alternative delivery instructions or a safe place to leave your package. We'll contact you before delivery.

Are your products fresh?

Yes! All our retail products are sourced fresh and packaged to maintain quality. We guarantee freshness on delivery.
