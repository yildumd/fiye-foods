Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Last orders for delivery are accepted 30 minutes before closing time.

Is there a minimum order for delivery?

Yes, we have a minimum order of ₦2,500 for delivery services to ensure efficient service.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We provide catering for events of all sizes. Contact us with your event details for a customized quote.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Order modifications are possible if we haven't started preparation. Please call us immediately at 0805 726 6953.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and will soon introduce online payment options.

Do you have vegetarian options?

While we specialize in Southern Nigerian cuisine which often includes meat and fish, we do offer vegetarian-friendly dishes like our vegetable soups and sides.
