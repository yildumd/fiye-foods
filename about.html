Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of Southern Nigeria to Abuja

Welcome to Fiye Foods

Founded with a passion for authentic Southern Nigerian cuisine, Fiye Foods brings the rich, aromatic flavors of the Niger Delta to the heart of Abuja. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the traditional recipes and cooking techniques passed down through generations.

At our location in Say Plaza, Utako, we've created a culinary haven where every dish tells a story of our heritage. From our signature Fiye Party Jollof to our authentic Banga Soup, each recipe is crafted with the same care and attention our grandmothers used in their kitchens.
